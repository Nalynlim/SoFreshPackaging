About Usเกี่ยวกับเรา

SoFresh Packaging is packaging wholesaler & solution provider who helps young brand owners turn ideas into reality with guaranteed high-quality packaging at lower MOQ (minimum order quantity) together with premium service and quality control.

We specialize in supporting startup, SME, and emerging beauty brands who want professional packaging with zero pressure on high number of MOQ.
